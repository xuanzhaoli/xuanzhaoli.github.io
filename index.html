Biography

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Genetics and Developmental Biology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, since Fall 2023. My research focuses on wheat genomics and the patterns of gene expression changes during distant hybridization. Prior to starting my Ph.D., I participated in several large-scale genomics projects as both a lead contributor and team member, including the rice pan-genome project and the locust genome project. I expect to graduate in 2027 and am currently seeking suitable career opportunities.
